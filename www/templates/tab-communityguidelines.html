Debatable Community Guidelines
We the Debatable Team would like to welcome you to our community and thank you for choosing our App.

Our community is built on core values such as respect, honesty, and equality. In order to ensure these values are upheld, users should conduct themselves according to the following guidelines:

Rule 1:
All posts should be respectful and profanity free. When profane language is being quoted in support of an argument, the quote should be censored.

Rule 2:
Though we understand some topics may be controversial, debate and arguments cannot be made to attack or target anyone based on race, religion, gender or anything else for that matter.

Rule 3:
Ridiculing other users for posts that they make is strictly not allowed as this taints our community's positive environment. Instead a counter argument should be made outlining the flaws in their argument.

Rule 4:
Intentional misrepresentation of facts in arguments is strictly forbidden.

Any user found in violation of any of the above rules will be sanctioned accordingly. Sanctions range from warnings to permanent ban.
